What is Avien 5.5?

Avien 5.5 is a body-care collection built around named routines. The range combines everyday cleansing and moisturizing with targeted options you can spot instantly from the label: Glycolic Acid, Salicylic Acid, Vitamin C, Kojic Acid, Alpha Arbutin + Niacinamide, and Retinol + Collagen.

General guidance: if you are new to exfoliating or retinol-based body care, patch test and introduce one "active" product at a time.

Why Avien 5.5

The Avien 5.5 line is designed to balance hydration, exfoliation, and glow care in simple daily routines. We stock the complete set so you can build a regimen that fits your skin goals.

Build a simple routine

Step 1: Cleanse

Pick a body wash that matches your goal (Smooth, Clarify, Brighten, Restore, or Wonder).

Step 2: Moisturize

Seal in moisture with a lotion from the range. Many customers pair within the same family name.

Step 3: Stay consistent

Introduce one active focus at a time and give your routine time to settle before switching.
